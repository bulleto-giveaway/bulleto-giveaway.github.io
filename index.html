Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to participate in a Bulleto program?

You can participate in a Bulleto program with at least 2 BBTC (value equal to 0.002 BTC) and 2 USDB (value equal to $2). This is the minimum participation cost, utilized in activating Slot 1 of A-Club.

How can I start? Do I need any training or technical knowledge?

There’s no eligibility or qualification requirement to participate in Bulleto. Anyone can find the helpful resources on Resources and learn everything about Bulleto through the official PDF or videos.

Where does my income come from?

Your income is generated from your downlines and uplines. Bulleto is a co-matrix smart contract that enables participants to earn from both directions. For further information about the flow of income through the slot matrix, please refer to the official PDF at Official PDF

What are the rewards? How much can I earn?

Bulleto rewards its participants with BBTC. The value of 1 BBTC is equal to the market value of 0.001 BTC. There’s no limit of how much an individual participant can earn. To learn more about the income potential, kindly refer to the official PDF at Official PDF
